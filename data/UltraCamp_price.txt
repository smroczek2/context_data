UltraCamp Pricing -
Minimal cost: $3,000/year for 750 registrations or 500 individuals depending on the type of plan they choose.
One-time setup fee: $1,000
Includes biographical import (name, birthdate, contact info in prescribed format)
Additional import of Donations and/or Reservations available upon request
Additional cost quoted on individual basis based on usability, time, and timeline for import
Pricing calculator available on UltraCamp's website: https://ultracampmanagement.com/home/pricing/
